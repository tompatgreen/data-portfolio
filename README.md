### Project 2: UK House Price Trends (Power BI)
**Goal:** Analyse UK house price trends across regions from 1970–2023  
**Skills:** Power BI, DAX, data modelling, time-series visualisation  
**Data:** UK government open data / Land Registry  

**Key Findings:**
- Average UK house price: £307,960
- London dominates — Kensington & Chelsea and Camden are the most 
  expensive regions by average price
- Prices show consistent upward growth since 1970, accelerating post-2000
- Growth rates vary significantly by region

### Project 3: Customer Churn Analysis (Power BI)
**Goal:** Identify key drivers of customer churn to inform retention strategy  
**Skills:** Power BI, DAX, data modelling, business insight  
**Data:** Telecom customer dataset (Kaggle)

**Key Findings:**
- Overall churn rate: 26.54% (1,869 of 7,043 customers)
- Month-to-month contract customers churn at ~42% — far higher than 
  annual or two-year contracts
- New customers (low tenure) are most at risk, with churn exceeding 45% 
  in the earliest tenure bracket
- Churn increases as monthly charges rise
